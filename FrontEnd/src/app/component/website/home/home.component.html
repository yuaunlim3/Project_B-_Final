What is Health Tracker?

Health Tracker is a comprehensive app designed to help you manage your health journey. Track calories, workouts, and see your progress toward achieving fitness goals.

Key Features (Available to All Users)

  • Calorie Tracking: Log your meals and monitor your daily calorie intake.
  • Workout Monitoring: Track your exercises and calories burned during workouts.
  • Progress Reports: View basic charts to measure your fitness progress.
  • Weather Forecasts: Get 3-day weather forecasts to plan your outdoor workouts.

Premium Features (Unlock Your Full Potential)

  • Advanced Analytics & Historical Data: Access your complete fitness history with unlimited date ranges and detailed trend analysis.
  • Comprehensive Nutritional Insights: Track detailed macronutrients (proteins, fats, carbs) and get breakdowns of your nutritional intake.
  • Complete Achievement System: Unlock and view all achievements to track your progress milestones and stay motivated.
  • Extended Weather Forecasts: Get 7-day weather forecasts to better plan your outdoor workout schedule.
  • Community Leaderboards: Compare your progress with others and compete in fitness challenges to stay motivated.
  • AI-Powered Health Tips: Receive personalized nutrition and workout recommendations based on your unique profile and goals.

How It Works

  1. Create an Account: Sign up to get started with your personal profile.
  2. Log Your Daily Activities: Enter your meals, workouts, and water intake.
  3. Track Your Progress: Use reports and charts to monitor your fitness journey.
  4. Achieve Your Goals: Stay motivated and achieve your health and fitness goals.
